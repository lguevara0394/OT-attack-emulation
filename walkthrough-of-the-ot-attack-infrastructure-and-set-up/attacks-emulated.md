# Attacks Emulated

**Attacks Emulated:**\
The attacks replicated in our experiment were ArcaneDoor and Night Dragon, both targeting OT\
infrastructure in a different way and approach. I leave the links to the MITRE mapping of both\
attacks here:\
ArcaneDoor: https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0046/\
Night Dragon: https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0002/\
Important thing to notice for ArcaneDoor we will need a router emulation we used GNS3 for it in\
order to best emulate the attack
